-What?-

Mindbook is basically a notebook which can read your thoughts and write them down for you if you allow it to.

-How?-

Generally, Mindbook works with the tiny detector placed in every single page, including the front page, and it reaches to your brain signals and writes according to the classified thoughts of yours as a result of the test which is done when you receive your Mindbook.

To learn more about the test as well as how it is connected to writing, read bellow.

-The Test-

The test is done to classify how you actually feel when you think on a specific thing. For instance, you would be probably happy while you are thinking about a good childhood memory. The detector inside the Ipad, which is the device that you are doing the test with, detects your general mood and reaches to the deepest part of your brain, where you usually store your thoughts. According to the gathered data, Mindbook verifies the result that it get's when it reads your thoughts. Therefore, the test is done to help Mindbook so it can write more accurately.
